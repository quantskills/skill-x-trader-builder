# Review Rules

Use these rules as the first-pass semantic filter.

## Keep

Use `keep` when a post contains:

- a clear thesis mechanism
- a company or asset placed in a chain, factor, or market structure
- evidence such as customer demand, capex, financial inflection, technical constraint, on-chain data, order flow, or policy
- risk or falsification logic
- follow-up metrics

## Keep But Deweight

Use `keep_deweighted` when:

- the trader states a preference, but gives only a short rationale
- the post is a broad basket
- the ticker appears in main text, but the mechanism is incomplete

## Deweight

Use `deweight` when:

- the post is a watchlist
- the idea is crowdsourced
- the author says they will start due diligence
- the ticker is only a candidate for future research

## Remove From Forward Signal

Use `remove_from_forward_signal_keep_as_track_record_context` when:

- the post says YTD returns, 10x, 100-1000%, called it, remember these names, track record, or thesis validation
- the post is useful for reputation analysis but not a new signal

## Delete From This Signal

Use `delete_from_this_signal` when:

- the ticker appears only in quoted text
- the ticker belongs to a question, prior post, another account, or comparison context

## Explainer

Use `keep_as_explainer_deweight` when:

- the post explains a crash, squeeze, macro event, or market structure
- there is no explicit long/short thesis

## Delete

Use `delete` when:

- the post is only a link
- there is no text or usable ticker
